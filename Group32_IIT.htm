5.      Overview

a.      Topic 

-          Development initiative to support English pronunciation.

b.      Motivation

-          There are three main factors that lead us to creating this wonderful application:

-          First of all is about the customers’ needs In the context of this contemporary society, English has become one of the most common languages in the world. As a result, the need to use and learn English is getting higher and higher daily.

-          Secondly is about the users. This sector has a very very large range of users due to the fact that learning English, especially improving pronunciation suitable for everybody so this application could serve every citizen from every country at every age.

-          Last but not least is about the market. Despite the lack of precise market data for English teaching applications, a market research platform named Markets & Markets predicts that the global educational technology market would grow from $8 billion to over $181 billion, from 2010 to 2025, respectively. Vietnam is one of the most promising markets for the growth of the EdTech sector in general and English teaching apps in particular, especially those that could help them to improve their pronunciation.

c.       Landscape​

-          Today, we have a lot of apps similar to ACCENT such as Cake, Elsa speaks, Learn English Daily, Accent training. Inside these applications, we appreciate Cake, Elsa speaks like competitors. Both apps appear sooner than ACCENT, so they have impressed the customer through the ads as well as the credit through the user's experiments. With those two points lthey account for the large number of customers. Moreover, Elsa Speak has various speaking exercises and Cake helps you study and pronounce new words through videos such as movies. However, ACCENT have all that feature and we have one more feature to compete with them, our app can record all time and we don’t need to open the app and find the topics like Elsa speak and cake to compare the record to the topic's record because the record will automatically send to the app which will analyze each word of pronunciation base on all the standard record we have and provide the analysis report instantly.

6.     Detailed Description

d.      Aims

-          Every member of our group has interacted with foreigners and regularly engages in conversation in English; in some cases, they may even use English to communicate with friends, family members, or teachers. For instance, when I play online games with gamers from various countries, I often speak to them in English. Through these encounters, our team identified a problem: some people have good abilities and a broad vocabulary but nevertheless have pronunciation issues. Because of this, our team discussed the idea of developing software that can help anyone pronounce words correctly no matter where they are and came up with the idea.

-          On the one hand, there are a ton of applications out there created particularly to help users learn English, many of which are not just highly acclaimed on well-known platforms but also by many users. However, It is time-consuming for people who work all day to use these tools because they all demand users to spend a lot of time studying each course. And even if they had the chance to sit down and take notes on such lectures, they could not always be very helpful because they could occasionally be sloppy estimates that fail to take into account the flaws of each person being used. Therefore, our team created this project to address that problem by creating a tool that may quickly boost proficiency and address each person's particular pronunciation issues without taking up too much of their time.

-          On the other hand, create a premium account for users who want a paid registration option to avoid being bothered by in-app advertisements and who need to improve both their pronunciation and their use of more professional languages. As a result, the cost of updating the app on 3 marketplaces like CH Play or the App Store is covered, and by providing team members with a source of money, the team is motivated to focus on creating more valuable software.

-          For a bigger goal, it is true that there are geographical differences in how English is sounded, which makes it challenging for speakers of other cultures to interact properly. Therefore, we are aiming to completely fix this problem so that it is easier for people from other countries to communicate with each other in special situations like traveling. As this period of globalization comes to a close, it also helps to promote international friendship and cross-cultural exchange.

j.        Risks

·         Project problem:

-          Many errors occurred for the web creation such as:

o   Getting some error when creating repositories and export website.io.

o   Missing to create the button, put images and some main paragraphs.

·         Software problems:

-          The app is sometimes unstable:

o   The machine is hot leading to overload and lag performance.

o   Login sometimes have error not getting username and password and login bug when no need to enter password.

·         Other problems from external factors:

-          Expected risks:

o   The input microphone quality of each device will be different, which affects the quality of the conversation.

o   The noise from the recording leads to the wrong recognition of the content of the dialogue.

o   While recording process, the microphone has a problem or is covered by the hand, leading to the quality of the dialogue.

o   Problem from devices: devices with little free storage or full capacity of the device when recording may overflow, resulting in loss of recording data.

o   Different pronunciations (accent of clients) and wrong identification of homonyms are also one of the reasons that affect the conversation.

o   The environment where the dialogue takes place also affects the recording (ambient noise: the sound of conversations from other people, the crying and playing of children, and other external influences such as wind sound, etc.)

-          Unexpected risks:

o   Problem from the application: The application works in two ways online and offline. For offline, it is possible to download data in the desired language of clients and it does not depend on the network connection. And for using online, when clients go to places where the network is poor or there is no network, using applications will interrupt and cannot access the data to convert and repair the conversation.

o   Battery life: The application must run continually in the background to record every user's discussion, which drains a lot of energy.

       V.       Project outcomes/Product Prototype

·         The function of ACCENT:

-          Voice Recording

-          Voice analysis and solution

-          Practice environment

·         How does it work?

-          Voice recording function: The app will work when it is provided permission. The users can set the time to begin and finish recording, or users can set it recording all day which depends on user’s demand. All the records will be deleted after 24 hours. In addition, users can connect the ACCENT record function with any record app that users trust.

-          Voice analysis and solution: ACCENT will record as well as send the record to the central system to compare with the standard record we have. After that the app will send the report containing the goods and the mistakes that users make. ACCENT will pronounce again and provide the illustration such as the way to use tongue, word stress, linking vowel sound which help the users to understand.

-          Practice environment: ACCENT has a lot of practice exercises containing various topics in type of videos or voice for users to explore and upgrade themselves. In every month or last week, ACCENT will send a report that totals your pronunciation mistakes and the things users have improved.

·         The limitation of ACCENT:

-          The record ability: It is very difficult to have a good record from users because the good record depends on the environment (ambient noise) the users live in and the microphone of the user’s phone.

-          The processing with long record: ACCENT is confident to analyze the long record quickly but with the record in the low Wi-Fi condition there will affect the processing time.

·         The plan to improve ACCENT:

-          Create the courses: After the app works stable, we are going to choose the users that have high points from the app to create the course for the new users to help them achieve their goal more quickly.

-          English group: ACCENT can be used like a group where everyone discusses only the topic related to English and shares tips.
